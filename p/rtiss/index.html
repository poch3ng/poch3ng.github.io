Abstract

This paper proposes a virtual production camera system, which allows photographers to connect the camera and the virtual environment quickly and capture the moving trajectory of the camera in reality through HTC VIVE and iPhone. It is synchronized with the virtual camera, allowing users to preview the synthesized result in real-time.
